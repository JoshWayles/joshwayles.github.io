Meowsteroids arcade game

Meowsteroids is a game I developed based on an introductory project in GameMaker to build an asteroids clone. I wanted to develop the project out further so I created my own sprites, heavily inspired by Game and Watch visuals, as well as SFX and music. Play for free here.
